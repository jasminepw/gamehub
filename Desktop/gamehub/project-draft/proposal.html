Group 6 Project Proposal

Problem/Data Domain

Our topic is gaming, but our intended audience will range from beginner to novice players. This site will allow users who are new to the community to discover games that can help them get started and veteran gamers to find new games they have not played yet.

The problem space that we are hoping to address are the limited resources available to introduce non-gamers to a relatively exclusive space. In addition, introducing the resources that can help increase exposure to smaller, newer video game creators and lesser-known games.

There have been some indirect attempts in order to address our problem. An attempt at the problem is gameslikefinder.com which suggests games based on games that you already play. In addition, Steam has features implemented that would allow you to find new games based on a recommendation list. Although both solutions have some features that address our problems such as a search for new games and recommendations, there is no way to introduce new to novice gamers into the relatively exclusive gaming community.

App Description

Users of our web application would be veteran gamers who are looking for new games to play or new gamers who are potentially interested in gaming and getting into a new hobby. This web application is for everyone of all ages and any background.

Users can use our web application to take a quiz based on their interests and what they are looking for in a game, then based on the results our web application will recommend games to the user. Users can login to save their quiz results to share with others, as well as rate the games that our app recommended to them. If users are interested in learning more about games they will be able to search and filter through the games that are listed on our web application.

The information that users will be viewing on our web application is their quiz results and the games that are recommended to them based on their results. Users can view these recommended games and the type of games that they are, what device they can be played on, if they are single or multiplayer games, and other descriptive topics. Users will also be able to see other’s ratings and quiz results.

Our web application can encourage people to get into a new hobby that may seem daunting and difficult to get into. For veteran gamers, it can allow people to discover new games based on their interests. For video game creators, they can get their game out to a larger audience and gain more exposure through our web application.

User Stories

  • Daniel (23) - As someone who has played games their entire life, I want to be able to find new games so that I can find different communities to hang out with.
  • Stella (22) - As someone who doesn’t play games very often and would like to get more into gaming, I want to be able to find games that I would enjoy playing and have a comfortable introduction to.
  • Joe (26) - As a gamer who is bored of the games I currently play, I want to find new games to play without wasting time and money testing different games I might like.
