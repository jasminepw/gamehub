Welcome to Game Hub!


Game Hub is an easy way for beginner and advanced gamers to be able to find new games to play based off of your interests! You can also be matched with others who have similar interests and got recommended the same games.

You can also search for games using filters or browse through our featured games below.

Take the quiz to find new games and connect with other gamers!

Featured Games

Unpacking

A game about the familiar experience of pulling possessions out of boxes and fitting them into a new home.

Slapshot Rebound

Slapshot: Rebound is a physics-based multiplayer game where you play an arcade style match of hockey.

Valorant

Valorant is a tactical shooting game involving two teams with 5 players in each team.

Overcooked 2

Cooperatively prepare and cook orders in teams of up to four players. Players gather, chop, and cook ingredients, combine them on plates, serve dishes, and wash dishes.

A Way Out

A Way Out is an exclusively co-op adventure where you play the role of one of two prisoners making their daring escape from prison

Unravel Two

Unravel Two is a platforming adventure game seen from a unique perspective, or two. Play as a pair of Yarnys to run, jump, and swing through platforming puzzles, foster friendship, and shed light on a world covered in shadow

NBA 2K

NBA2K allows the player to compete in basketball games with the current NBA season's players and teams. Players can even create customizable athletes!

Boba Tale

You are now a proud owner of a bubble tea shop! Start your adventure by making drinks for the folks in town, and upgrade your shop and drink recipes to build your boba empire
